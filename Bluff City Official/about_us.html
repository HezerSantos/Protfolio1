Bluff City Pet Hospital had its beginnings in 1985 as a wellness

veterinary clinic. Our mission is to celebrate the very precious

bond that people have with their pets through exceptional and

relational veterinary care. We do this in a caring way. We truly

value the relationship we have with you and your beloved pet.

A veterinary health care team of over 30 dedicated people who

truly care for you and your pets

We greet you at the door and help you inside

We take the time to explain what’s needed and what’s not

We will offer treatment options for your pet and help you make the

best decision for your pet's needs

We give back to our community often through donations and

volunteer efforts

We light a candle in the lobby while families say goodbye to their

pet and donate to the Good Samaritan Fund in their memory

We care for our team first so that they can best care for you and

your pet

Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement through

education and team training
